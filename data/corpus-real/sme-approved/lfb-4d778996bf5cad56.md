## Question

What are your backup procedures?

## Answer

Automated daily snapshots of all customer data are retained for 35 days. Weekly snapshots are retained for 12 months. Cross-region replication copies each snapshot to the secondary region. Restore drills are performed quarterly. [dr_bcp_overview]
