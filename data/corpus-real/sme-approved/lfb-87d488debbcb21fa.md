## Question

Is MFA supported and which factors?

## Answer

MFA is supported for all user tiers and can be enforced by administrator policy. Supported factors: TOTP authenticator apps, WebAuthn/FIDO2 hardware keys, and SMS (not recommended for high-assurance environments). Hardware keys are required for administrative roles in the Enterprise tier. [sso_mfa_guide]
