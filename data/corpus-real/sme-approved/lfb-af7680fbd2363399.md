## Answer

Security incidents are triaged by our 24×7 Security Operations Center within 15 minutes of detection. A named Incident Commander follows the NIST 800-61 framework. Customers affected by a confirmed security incident are notified within 72 hours with scope, impact, and remediation status. [soc2_cert_2025]
